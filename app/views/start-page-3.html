Get a firearm certicate

Use this service to:

  • Get permission to possess and use firearms

You must be 18 years old or above to use this service.

Applying takes around 20 minutes.

Start now

Before you start

You can also apply by post.

This service requires you to use Verify to prove your identiy.

You will be asked for permission to contact your GP.

You will need two referees that can be contacted by local police.

You will need to upload a passport photograph.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Getting a firearm licence can take up 8 weeks.
